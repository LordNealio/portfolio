# Featured Projects

Six projects carry the homepage "Selected Work" grid; **Creation OS** anchors the
Vision page as the connecting thesis. The rest live in the filterable archive with
honest status labels, so featured weight signals genuine strength — not everything
shown equally.

## The featured six

| Rank | Project | Why it leads | Status shown |
|------|---------|--------------|--------------|
| 1 | **MindVault** | The flagship. Live, deep, and the clearest expression of the "private core + opt-in AI" thesis. Range: journaling, habits, publishing, inquiry. | Live |
| 2 | **Mirror** | Most distinctive point of view. A principled, privacy-by-architecture product — proves conviction, not just capability. | Live |
| 3 | **LegacyBridge** | Most emotionally resonant and most complete build. Broadens the story from "self" to "family / community." | Prototype |
| 4 | **WorkWrite** | The B2B / operator story with a named pilot target (Din Tai Fung). Shows Justin can define a product for an organization, not just an individual. | Prototype |
| 5 | **The 7 Temples Tour** | The cinematic, creative-director showcase. Visually the most ambitious — signals taste and range beyond utility apps. | Concept |
| 6 | **E.Manual** | The education story, tied directly to Justin's teaching background. Native mobile (Expo) adds platform range. | Prototype |

**Balance check:** 2 live · 3 prototype · 1 concept · spans self, family, work,
culture, and education · covers web, PWA, and native mobile. No single category
dominates, and every credibility tier is represented honestly.

## The Vision anchor

**Creation OS** is deliberately *not* in the featured product grid — presenting an
architecture as a shippable app would overstate it. Instead it headlines the
**Vision** page as the idea connecting everything, which is both more accurate and
more compelling (it reframes the portfolio from "a pile of apps" to "one thesis,
many interfaces").

## Deliberately archived (not featured)

K-Source Vault, SoundWorld Studio, S4US1McGamer, RapGod, MindWrite (origin),
Everythang, Pimps' Paradise. These add range and depth on the Work page but would
dilute the homepage. MindWrite (origin) is worth surfacing in conversation as the
literal starting point of the whole journey.

## How to change the featured set
In `src/data/projects.ts`, flip `featured: true/false` on any record. The homepage
grid, counts, and "Selected Work" section update automatically.
